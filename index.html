OUR STORY

One studio. Any space. One mission.

Space Projects 236 was founded by Brandon Hill, a software professional with over 15 years of experience across development, product strategy, and digital solutions. After designing and building Gimmzi — a full-scale loyalty and rewards SaaS platform — from the ground up, Brandon realized the same process, tools, and expertise could help other entrepreneurs and businesses bring their ideas to life. Space Projects 236 was born from that mission: to be the builder that others need.

Our portfolio includes Gimmzi, a live loyalty and rewards platform, alongside client sites for Shoreline Plumbing and CM Financialz. LessMeds, a polypharmacy management app, is currently in development. Every product we build starts with an idea and ends with something real.
